Measures of Position: Deciles

Understanding Deciles

Deciles divide a sorted dataset into exactly 10 equal parts. Because it is divided into 10 parts, the denominator in our formula changes to 10.

$D_k = LB + \left( \frac{\frac{kN}{10} - cf_b}{f_{D_k}} \right) i$
